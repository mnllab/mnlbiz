Privacy Policy for Tab Grabber Last updated: July 1, 2025 This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service. The "Tab Grabber" extension for Google Chrome does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. The extension accesses the text content of your open web pages ("Website Content") only when you explicitly activate it by clicking the "Save All Tabs" button. This data is processed locally on your computer to create a text file. This file is then saved directly to your computer via the browser's download function. No data, including website content or your browsing history, is ever sent to our servers or any third party. All processing is done on your local machine. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, You can contact us.
