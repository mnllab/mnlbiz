Privacy Policy for Tab Grabber

Last updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service.

The "Tab Grabber" extension for Google Chrome does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users.

The extension accesses the text content of your open web pages ("Website Content") only when you explicitly activate it by clicking the "Save All Tabs" button. This data is processed locally on your computer to create a text file. This file is then saved directly to your computer via the browser's download function.

No data, including website content or your browsing history, is ever sent to our servers or any third party. All processing is done on your local machine.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, You can contact us.

Privacy Policy for Reference Extractor

Last updated: July 1, 2025

The "Reference Extractor" extension for Google Chrome requires a user-provided API key to function. This key is stored locally and securely on your device using `chrome.storage.sync` and is only used to make requests to the AI API you have configured.

The extension accesses the text content of your open web pages only when you explicitly activate it. This content is sent to the AI service for processing and is not stored or collected by the extension itself.

No personal data, browsing history, or API keys are ever sent to our servers. All processing is handled between your browser and the AI service you provide the key for.

Privacy Policy for ClearCut News

Last updated: July 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines our procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our service.

The "ClearCut News" extension for Google Chrome requires a user-provided Google AI API key to perform its core function. This key, along with your preferred settings (like save mode), is stored securely and locally on your device using `chrome.storage.sync`. We do not have access to this key.

The extension accesses the content of your active web page only when you explicitly activate it by clicking the "Extract Article From This Page" button. This content is sent directly from your browser to the Google AI service for the sole purpose of generating a summary. The content of the web pages you visit is not stored or collected by the extension itself.

We want to be clear: No personal data, Browse history, or your API key is ever sent to our servers. All data processing occurs either locally on your computer or directly between your browser and the Google AI service you provide the key for.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us.
