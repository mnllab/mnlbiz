MNL Chief Consultant, Jang-Gil Kim

MNL is a business consulting brand for those who want to achieve tangible results through entrepreneurship. CEO Jang-Gil Kim is a research professor at Seoul National University College of Engineering and has been a hands-on startup education/consulting expert for over 10 years, providing close support from item validation to market research, prototype development, business plan establishment, and fundraising. Anyone can dream of starting a business, but actually entering the market and making money is a completely different story.

MNL turns business ideas into concrete models, establishes execution strategies, and provides essential advice and feedback for companies to move to the next level. It's okay if you don't have startup experience. It's okay if the direction is unclear. Starting from here, you can also become a successful CEO. We provide a path for those who want to become CEOs and practical methods for those who want to create profits. Not desk theory, but startups that work with markets and customers. You can start now, here.
